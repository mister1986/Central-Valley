Common questions

Answers Before Your Consultation

How much does full-arch actually cost — total, no surprises? +
Our All-on-4 starts at $11,900 per arch — includes IV sedation, all extractions, 4 titanium implants, bone graft, and same-day provisional teeth. Financing starts at $399/month with 0% options. You'll receive a complete personalized quote at your free consultation. No hidden fees, ever.
Will I feel any pain during or after the procedure? +
No. All procedures are performed under IV sedation — you won't feel a thing during surgery. Most patients are genuinely surprised by how manageable recovery is. Over 90% rate post-op discomfort at 3/10 or lower. We provide a full recovery kit and 24/7 post-op support.
I have significant bone loss — am I still a candidate? +
Very likely yes. Dr. B specializes in complex cases including patients turned away elsewhere. Advanced 3D CBCT imaging assesses your bone precisely, and bone grafting techniques can solve most situations. Over 90% of consultations result in a viable plan.
Why not just get dentures — aren't they cheaper? +
Dentures are cheaper upfront but most patients regret them. They slip, cause pain, accelerate bone loss, and need replacing every 7–10 years. Implants fuse with your jaw, last 20+ years, feel completely natural, and preserve your facial structure. Long-term, implants cost less and deliver infinitely more.
What exactly happens at the free consultation? +
You'll be greeted by our team and taken for a painless 3D panoramic scan. Then Dr. B reviews your case personally, walks through every option, and presents a personalized treatment plan with full transparent pricing — all in one visit. Zero obligation, zero pressure to proceed.
