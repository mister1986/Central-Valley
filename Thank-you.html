Common questions

Answers Before Your Consultation

What makes dental implants so great?
Titanium implants cannot decay and fuse with your bone biologically — becoming a lifelong root. They also preserve jaw bone and prevent it from dwindling away.
Why can't I just get dentures instead?
Dentures are cheaper upfront but the majority of patients end up hating them — they cause pain, discomfort, and accelerate bone loss. Most wish they'd chosen implants from the start.
Will my procedure hurt?
No. We perform all procedures under IV sedation — you won't feel a thing. Post-op pain can be significantly reduced with prescription pain management we provide.
Is the 3D scan painful?
Definitely not. The machine simply rotates around your head taking digital pictures. It's completely non-invasive and much more accurate than traditional X-rays.
What happens at the appointment?
You'll be greeted by our staff, filled in on your situation, and taken for a painless 3D Panoramic Scan. Then Dr. B reviews your case and presents a personalized plan — all in one visit.
How much do dental implants cost?
Every procedure is different. Our All-on-4 starts at $11,900/arch. To get an accurate estimate we need to see your scans — that's exactly what the free consultation is for.
