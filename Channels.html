How we count a qualified lead: A lead is qualified when it reaches an actual appointment touchpoint — Scheduled Appointment, No Show, Didn't Show Up, Treatment Proposal, Started Treatment, or Closed. "New Booking" (form submitted but appointment not yet confirmed) is NOT counted. Revenue uses your CRM's Lead Value when present, and a $2,934 default per new patient for Started Treatment without an explicit value.
